ANSSATZ invents, designs, manufactures and sells intelligent electric vehicles with genuinely innovative technology.

Its objective is to generate a significant and positive impact in the electromobility industry around the world by developing breakthroughs in mechanics, electronics, industrial design, sustainability, artificial intelligence and communications.

With great growth plans in the coming years, the team seeks to push the electromobility industry to new radical milestones and serve as the platform of the future.
